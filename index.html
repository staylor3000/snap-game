Play Snap Card Game Online Free

PlaySnap is a free online version of the classic Snap card game — perfect for a quick 5-minute break, a commute, or any time you have a few minutes to kill. Play instantly against the CPU with no login or download required.

How to Play Snap

Each player takes turns flipping cards onto a central pile. When two cards of the same value appear on top of the pile, be the first to hit SNAP! to win the pile. The player who collects all the cards wins. In PlaySnap, you compete against a CPU opponent across 10 levels of increasing difficulty.

Controls

Click Flip Card (or hit the Space bar) to play your card, then hit SNAP! (or press Enter) when you spot a matching pair. React fast — the CPU gets quicker as you level up!
